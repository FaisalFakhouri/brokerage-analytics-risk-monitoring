## LinkedIn Project Description

I built a Power BI brokerage analytics and risk-monitoring platform designed around a complete analytical workflow: from executive monitoring to account-level investigation.

The project includes six pages covering executive KPIs and trends, client and account analysis, trading performance, risk monitoring, alerts and compliance, and account investigation.

One of the main features is an account-level drill-through workflow. An account identified in a management attention list or risk review queue can be opened in a dedicated investigation page, where connected KPIs and visuals recalculate under the selected account context.

The project uses Power BI, DAX, semantic-model relationships, filter context, PBIP project files, Git, and GitHub.
